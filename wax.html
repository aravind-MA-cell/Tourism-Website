Kanniyakumari Wax Museum



It looks like a hall where the world’s famous celebrities have come together; hanging out with their fans for photoshoots and having a great time. The wax statues at the Wax Museum in Kanniyakumari is an interesting place to visit.



Located inside the Baywatch amusement park in Kanniyakumari, the wax museum is designed in line with the world-famous Madame Tussaud’s in London. Though not a grand one with so much of exhibits when compared to various other bigger wax museums in the world, Kanniyakumari Wax Museum still has enough in to excite a visitor. Kids will especially love the museum and a chance to see figures of their dearest stars up close. Historical figures, Indian and international movie actors, political figures, sports personalities etc. are included in the list. Some of the most noted celebrities in the hall include Mahatma Gandhi, Mother Teresa, Indira Gandhi, Atal Bihari Vajpayee, Dr. Manmohan Singh, Amithabh Bacchan, Arnold Schwarzenegger, Shahrukh Khan Jackie Chan and so goes the list.

Kanyakumari Station, about 1.3 km away
