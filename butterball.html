Krishna's Butterball



And at the very heart of Mamallapuram lies a marvel, a wonder, a testament to the grandeur of the past - the incomparable, the indomitable, the ethereal, Krishna's Butterball.

The town of Mahabalipuram, a paradise on Earth, is where the ancient and the modern coexist in perfect harmony. It is where the past and the present come together to create a tapestry of beauty and wonder, a tapestry that is woven with the thread of legend and embellished with the gold of tradition.

This colossal orb of granite is a sphere of such perfect balance and proportion that it seems as if it were crafted by the gods themselves. It sits there, upon its tiny pedestal, defying the laws of physics and nature, as if held aloft by the very breath of the divine.

It is not just a marvel of nature and science; it is also a symbol of the playful nature of the god Krishna and a reminder of the delicate balance between the mortal and the immortal. And as you stand before this wonder, you cannot help but feel a sense of awe and reverence for the power of the divine and the beauty of the natural world.

Mahabalipuram is also a treasure trove of ancient temples, sculptures, and carvings that tell the stories of gods and goddesses, kings and queens, and the everyday lives of the people of the past.
