Para Sailing




Soar like a bird and float like a butterfly and cherish the ecstasy and fun of flying. Tamil Nadu offers some of the safest parasailing spots in the country that will enable you to savour some of the breathtaking views of its iconic landscapes. Gear up and float away in the sky with wind in your hair. Experience the lightness of a bird and flirt with the clouds while being enchanted by the beauty of the landscape beneath you. Head to any of the parasailing spots across Tamil Nadu and soar like birds with wind in your hair and an aerial view of your favourite destination.

If you are daring enough to venture into the sky, Chennai is where you should head to. With several spots offering parasailing and paramotoring facilities, Chennai has all facilities to help you answer the call of the clouds. Parasailing over Marina Beach is the most popular choice of all. Fly along with seagulls over the crashing waves of the Bay of Bengal with a splendid evening view of sunset and bustling beach beneath you. Chennai has facilities for paramotoring also. Hitch a joyride with trained and certified professionals, and enjoy the thrill and joy of weaving in and out of the clouds like a bird.

Ariyaman Beach in Rameswaram is another destination where one can quench your desire to soar away into clouds like Icarus. The pristine beach on the Palk Bay of Rameshwaram is an underexplored destination that offers numerous adventure activities. Along with wind surfing and boating, the beach has facilities for parasailing over the crashing blue waves of the bay.
